  1. Do these values differ from the estimates from the first part of the assignment? What is the impact of imputing missing data on the estimates of the total daily number of steps?

Since the missing values were only substituted with 0s, the only difference can be appreciated in the histogram, where the frequency of 0s skyrocketed in the new dataset. It could be expected that the 0s had an effect on the mean number of steps taken per day, but since those days where data exists have (if they have at all) very few NAs, the 0s have a negligible impact on the result. If I had used another strategy to fill the NAs, then the means could have suffered some modifications (obviously more notorious in those days with no data at all). Only those medians with an NA value would have changed.
